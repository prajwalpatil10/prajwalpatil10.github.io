Technical Publication Services

At DGS we take pride in the quality of documentation that is clear, concise and precise. We combine the latest development software and the most efficient document design and management techniques for the best outcome.

Domain expertise

  • Operator Manuals
  • Service Manuals
  • Students Training Guide
  • Instructors Guide
  • Parts Catalogue
  • Scientific & Technical Manuals (STM)
  • Troubleshooting Guides

We have extensive practical publishing and editorial experience in handling every facet of production and editing projects for our clients. Our publishing and authoring support spans the complete lifecycle of your product right from conceptualization to its in-service life. Our knowledge of the industry standards helps in creating documents that meet the industry-specific certification requirements.

Our team also has extensive experience in wide array of illustration, authoring and translation tools to make sure your productivity is enhanced. We understand your needs for improving your product line, standardizing processes, and customizing applications. We provide timely and high-quality documentation across various sectors.
